ABOUT

Ayurveda - a conjugation of two Sanskrit words 'ayus', meaning 'life' and 'veda',meaning 'science', thus Ayurveda translates as the 'science of life'. It’s knowledge originated in India more than 5,000 years ago and is often referred to as “Mother of All Healing.” It comes from the age-old Vedic culture and was passed on for many thousands of years in an oral tradition from accomplished masters to their fellow disciples.

The progress toward balance and health can come slowly and gradually but sticking to the wellness regime can be beneficial in the long term. Sometimes, old habits can die hard but bringing about small changes here and there in your daily lifestyle slowly can bring about balance in your life and enrich your mind & body balances.
